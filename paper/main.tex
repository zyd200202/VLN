    $0.39{\to}0.89$). \textbf{(b)} The same restoration moves success by only

    HM3D ObjectNav episode under low-light severity~$4$; bright path traces
    the real rendered trajectory. \emph{Annotations:} aggregate statistics
    on the $N{=}300$ main split---the agent reaches within one metre of the
    goal in $62\%$ of episodes but succeeds in only $37\%$, a $26$-point
    reach-to-success gap that no perception-side intervention closes.
    \textbf{(d)} The full nine-arm intervention sweep (companion to
    Tab.~\ref{tab:sweep}): markers are paired $\Delta$SR vs.\ the frozen
    baseline, bars are bootstrap $95\%$ CIs, $*$ marks McNemar
    $p{<}0.05$. Every deployable arm is either indistinguishable from
    zero or \emph{significantly negative}; the only significant positive
    is the non-deployable per-frame appearance oracle, capped at
    $+0.057$.}
